**Where Python scripts meet runner's high.**

The **Humblebrag Dashboard** is a fully automated, open-source analytics tool for Strava. It is designed for runners and cyclists who want to visualize their year-over-year progression, track gamified challenges (like climbing Mt. Everest), and justify their pizza consumption through data.

![Dashboard Preview](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ak91hu/HumblebragDashboard/main/hbd.png)

## Why use this?
* **It's Free:** No premium Strava subscription required.
* **It's Automated:** A robot fetches your data every morning.
* **It's Gamified:** Convert calories to beer, pizza, and burgers.
